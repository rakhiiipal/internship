Ministry of Ayush

About Survey

The mainstreaming of Ayush systems is one of the core strategies in the National Health Mission (NHM) which seeks to provide accessible, affordable, and quality health care to the population and particularly to the vulnerable sections by correcting the deficiencies in the existing health care delivery system and by integrating health care facilities with the ongoing vertical health care programmes. Under NHM, a large number of Ayush facilities have been set up in PHCs, CHCs and DHs and Ayush Physicians have been posted in these facilities. The objective of the survey is to understand the roles and responsibilities of Ayush doctors working under NHM facilities.
